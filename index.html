Welcome to the 1maginary.online Wiki!

Explore information about the history of 1maginary, artists that have been on 1*RADIO, creators and more.

1maginary.online is an independent creative collaborative project that aims to platform artists and produce content. These articles serve to document and archive everything you would want to know about the 1maginary universe.

Permissions

To create pages or edit the wiki, you must have an 1maginary account.

Users can only create 1 page every 6 hours. This is to prevent potential spam.

Wiki Etiquette

  • Citation is recommended if possible.
  • Basic information that is public and relevant to their role in the 1* universe should be the limit. No doxxing.
  • Do not harass, attack, or demean any users, artists or moderators.
  • Provide relevant discussions in comments.
  • If you spam pages or are percieved to be acting with malice, you will be banned and your account will be disabled.

Reporting Problems
